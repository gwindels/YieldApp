Philosophy

Built like an
instrument.

Most training apps optimize for engagement. Yield optimizes for signal. The difference is everything you don't see on screen.

01
One thing at a time
Each screen shows exactly what the current moment requires. Nothing is surfaced speculatively. There is no dashboard.
02
Purpose-driven structure
A protocol isn't a list of workouts. It's a plan with defined objectives, measurable indicators, and a target date.
03
Measurement, not motivation
Yield doesn't encourage you. It records precisely what happened. Consistent measurement outperforms motivation every time.
